This study integrates three complementary genomic datasets from APP/PS1 (APP/PS1deltaE9) mouse brains treated with Bexarotene (RXR agonist) or Vehicle for 10 consecutive days: single-nucleus ATAC-seq for chromatin accessibility, scRNA-seq for transcriptomics, and RXR ChIP-seq for direct binding validation. The snATAC-seq and ChIP-seq datasets were newly generated for this study, while the scRNA-seq data were originally published by Saibro-Girardi et al. (2025) and reanalyzed here for cross-modal integration.

Dataset 1 (GEO: GSE283906) — snATAC-seq: Single-nucleus ATAC-seq chromatin accessibility profiles from brains of 5-month-old APP/PS1 mice treated with Bexarotene (n = 5) or Vehicle (n = 5), totaling 10 samples. Libraries were prepared using the 10x Genomics Chromium Next GEM Single Cell ATAC Kit v2 and sequenced on Illumina NovaSeq S2 PE100. After quality filtering, 61,353 nuclei were profiled across major brain cell types including astrocytes, microglia, neurons, oligodendrocytes, endothelial cells, macrophages, pericytes/vascular smooth muscle cells, and choroid plexus epithelial/ependymal cells. This dataset is the primary source for chromatin accessibility analysis, differentially accessible peak identification, motif enrichment (chromVAR), and transcription factor footprinting (TOBIAS). Associated with BioProject PRJNA1196019.

Dataset 2 (GEO: GSE289039) — ChIP-seq: RXR ChIP-seq from APP/PS1 mouse brain tissue treated with Bexarotene (n = 2) or Vehicle (n = 2), totaling 4 samples. Libraries were prepared using TruSeq ChIP Sample Prep Kit and sequenced on Illumina HiSeq 2000. Peak calling with MACS2 identified 11,957 significant RXR-binding peaks. This dataset was used to independently validate RXR binding at differentially accessible chromatin regions identified by snATAC-seq and to identify overlapping ATAC-seq/ChIP-seq peaks for cis-regulatory element annotation. Associated with BioProject PRJNA1220684.

Dataset 3 (GEO: GSE283905) — scRNA-seq: Single-cell RNA-seq from APP/PS1 mouse brains treated with Bexarotene (n = 4) or Vehicle (n = 4), totaling 8 samples. Libraries were sequenced on Illumina NovaSeq 6000 and processed with Cell Ranger v7.0 and Seurat v4.2.0/v4.2.1. After quality filtering, 37,634 cells were retained. These data were originally generated by Saibro-Girardi et al. (2025, bioRxiv) and reanalyzed in this study for integrative multi-omic analysis. Reanalysis was limited to normalization and differential expression procedures necessary for cross-modal integration with the snATAC-seq and ChIP-seq datasets. Associated with BioProject PRJNA1196010.

The three datasets are complementary modalities from the same experimental paradigm (same mouse model, age, treatment schedule). The snATAC-seq (GSE283906) and scRNA-seq (GSE283905) datasets were integrated using ArchR with unconstrained integration to transfer cell-type labels. The ChIP-seq (GSE289039) serves as an independent validation layer. All three converge in the integrative analysis linking RXR binding to cis-regulatory elements and transcriptional output across brain cell types.
